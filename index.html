Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals to incorporate stretching into their daily lives, helping them reduce stress, prevent injuries, and improve mobility.

  • Accessibility: We are committed to making high-quality stretching guidance available to everyone, regardless of their fitness level or schedule.
  • Innovation: Continuously improving our app with the latest research and user feedback to provide the best stretching experience possible.
  • Wellness: Promoting a holistic approach to health that integrates physical flexibility with mental well-being
  • Innovation: Staying at the forefront of technology and fitness research, we continuously enhance our app with the latest advancements. From personalized AI-driven stretching plans to interactive features, we strive to offer cutting-edge tools that elevate your stretching experience.

Privacy Policy Details

This Privacy Policy describes how Mr_Good_Bear ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our services ("Services"), including our mobile application QuickStretch: Flex & Ease.


1. Information We Collect

Personal Data: We do not collect or store any personal data on our servers.

Third-Party Services: Our app includes third-party services such as AdMob by Google for the purpose of serving advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information, such as device identifiers, to provide personalized ads.


2. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect personal data, we do not use your information for any purposes other than those related to third-party services like AdMob.


3. Third-Party Services

AdMob may collect and use data under its own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing AdMob's Privacy Policy for more information on how they handle your data:

AdMob (Google) Privacy Policy


4. Data Security

We prioritize your data security. Since we do not store any personal data, risks associated with unauthorized access to personal data are minimized. However, third-party services like AdMob have their own security measures in place.


5. Your Privacy Rights

Opt-Out of Personalized Ads: You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
